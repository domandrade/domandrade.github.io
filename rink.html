Why this is here

A big part of event ops is being the person who sees the whole game — where's the crowd flow about to jam, which vendor is slipping, which exit needs a hand. Hockey taught me that read. So did dawn shifts on a grounds crew, where the course has to look perfect before the first tee time even knows you were there.
